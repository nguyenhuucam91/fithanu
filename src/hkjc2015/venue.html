Hanoi University



Hanoi University (Abbreviation: HANU; Vietnamese: Đại học Hà Nội) (formerly Hanoi University of Foreign Studies), established in 1959 in Hanoi, is now, a leading institution in foreign language training and research.

Hanoi University offers bachelor's degrees in 10 foreign languages including English, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian, French, German, Spanish and Italia. Among these, Masters and Ph.Ds are offered in Russian, English, French and Chinese. Besides, the following foreign languages are offered as second foreign languages: Bulgarian, Hungarian, Polish, Chzech, Slovak, Rumanian and Thai.

Since 2002, the University has offered following Bachelor Programs taught in English: Business Management, Tourism Management, Finance-Banking and Accounting, International Studies, Computer Science and Information Technology.

Foreign students make up for 10% of the total student population and they are enrolled either for Business, Technology or Vietnamese Studies.
